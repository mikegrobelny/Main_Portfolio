Process

Design is everywhere.
I see it as the journey toward a realised product, service or even large building or town. There are guides and road maps for designing all kinds of products from physical to digital and eveything in between. Having tried a wide variety of methods during my education and work as a designer, my process currently aligns closely with Design Thinking as articulated by IDEO and takes a lot inspiration from Don Normans - Design of Everyday Things.
