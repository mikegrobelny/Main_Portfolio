About

Having a background in industrial product design, I have begun to move into digital design and development by applying the design process I am familiar with to websites and digital products.
My coding experience began with programming machinery to create products that first existed in the digital world of Computer Aided Drafting or CAD. This was visual programming with 3D models, G-Code and parametric modelling for machine movements.
My focus now is on web development, to hone my skills as a developer and look for opportunities to combine my experience of 3D modelling with digital interfaces.

When I'm not coding and creating, I'm usually found either in or on the ocean, floating, splashing, surfing and fishing.
